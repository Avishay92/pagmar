Fontune is an experimental project
that explores the synthesis of sound
and typography and provides a unique way
to design the shape of a letter
by using musical parameters.



Fontune is a senior project, designed as part of the B.Design studies
at the Visual Communication Department of the Design Faculty at Hit -
Holon Institute of Technology.

Designed by Avishay Ram
Coded by Roni Shaham, Matan Yossef, Liron Shalev
Guided by Golan Gafni
